Overview

Education and Experience

I am a student at the National University of Singapore (NUS), enrolled in the Bachelor of Computing in Computer Science (Honours) programme, specialising in the Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence tracks. I'm currently in an intern role as the Software Developer at Acumino, an early-stage venture-builder startup that develops shared services. Currently, I'm co-developing a digital human HR interviewer with advanced components like Cloud Architecture, NLP, chatbots, REST API and a backend web system.

Design + Development

My most notable software side projects include an Android app made to enhance visual education in 3D chemistry using Augmented Reality, a website that predicts urban flooding in Fanno Creek with 98% accuracy, an expense tracker to help business-owners and a productivity chatbot to help you keep track of your deadlines and events. I've also done research in using Augmented Reality to improve e-commerce and promote social engagement (awarded the Nanyang Research Programme Gold Award) and in Mixed Integer Nonlinear Programming via Simulated Annealing.

I've dipped my toes into learning C# with Unity 3D and some full-stack web dev frameworks (NodeJS + React + Firebase) through my side and internship projects.
